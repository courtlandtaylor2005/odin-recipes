Fried Chicken

Picture of Crispy Fried Chicken

The recipe allows for crispy golden pieces of fried chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken Pieces
  • Buttermilk
  • Seasoned Flour Mixture
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Clean pieces of chicken
  2. Marinate chicken pieces in buttermilk and spices for 2 hours
  3. Dredge chicken in seasoned flour
  4. Fry chicken in 350 degress Ferenheight until internal temperature reaches 165 degress Ferenheight
