Hamburger

Picture of a Juicy Hamburger

The recipe allows for a juicy hamburger

Ingredients

  • 80/20 chunk of Ground Beef Patty
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Toasted Bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Condiments
  • Cheese(optional)

Steps

  1. Shape ground beef into patty to your liking
  2. Season meet with your sesoning
  3. Cook on a high heat grill or pan for 3-5 minutes per side
  4. Leet rest for 5 minutes before eating
